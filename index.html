  • I started out with House music /Hardcore around the year 1992 with some of the early software Screamtracker and Fasttracker.

    Later i started to like all the hardware and began to buy some synthesizers, compressors etc and a mixdesk and the sound slowly inproved. Over the years the computers became alot better and i started experimenting with softsynths like the Virus Powercore and as a DAW i was working in Steinberg Cubase/Nuendo and Propellorhead Reason. And i started with main stream Dance music and Trance. In the year 2004 i released my first record as a collab with my good friend Daniel Wanrooy. Later on more releases followed.

    About 3 years ago music for film came on my path and my interest for Dance music was slowly fading to the background.

    I still like the sound of the Dance music so these influences are still there in the music i make now, but i like a good balance of real instruments and synthesizers.
